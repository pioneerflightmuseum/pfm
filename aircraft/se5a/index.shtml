Latest Updates

In their copious free time, a couple of the Team Fokker members have started working on a worthy opponent to the Fokker D.VII - an SE5a! This project is literally just getting started. Here's a picture of Tom Gaylord and Bill Broussard standing with some wing ribs and the jigs used to make them. The fuselage is also under construction, in Oklahoma, by Jack Kearby in Tulsa.
