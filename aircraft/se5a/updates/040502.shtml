SE5a Update - 2 May 2004

While we here at Kingsbury have been working mainly on the Fokker D.VII, other volunteers have been working on a reproduction SE5a. That work has come to fruition, and the components have been delivered to the Aerodrome. We received the fuselage, landing gear, and tail surfaces recently. The quality of the work is very good, as can be seen from this picture of the rudder bar!
