Canuck Update - 5 September 2002

Although work has slowed down a bit, it hasn't stopped. Vern Hatch has been working on the Canuck, and his progress is obvious! It's now painted except for the logo (we're going to make it look like a barnstormer's airplane from the 1930s), and pretty much ready for final assembly. The only major piece missing is the engine. We have a Curtiss OX-5 slated for this plane, we just have to find the time to rebuild it! Once that's done, first flight should follow quickly.
