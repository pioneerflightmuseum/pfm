Canuck Update - 31 January 2004

After a great deal of fiddling around with it, we finally decided that the engine just wasn't going to work. So, we pulled it off the airplane and completely disassembled it. It turned out that several areas needed work, including the valve seats. They've been re-ground with the correct angles, and the engine is ready for re-assembly. Once again, we hope to have it flying by AirFair.
