Welcome to the Pioneer Flight Museum! The PFM is a group that is dedicated to the preservation, restoration, and reproduction of aircraft and other historical artifacts from the beginning of flight to the early years of WWII. We are a living history museum, and keep as many of our aircraft, vehicles, and artifacts in working order as we can. Download a PDF Membership application and join us! Donations are also gratefully accepted!

The Pioneer Flight Museum is located at Old Kingsbury Aerodrome in Kingsbury, Texas. Tours are available by appointment, please call (830) 639-4162.
