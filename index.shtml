Welcome to the Pioneer Flight Museum, a 501c3 nonprofit organization dedicated to the preservation, restoration, and reproduction of aircraft and historical ground vehicles from the beginning of flight to the early years of WWII. We are a living history museum, and keep as many of our aircraft, vehicles, and artifacts in working order as we can. Download a PDF Membership application and join us! Donations are also gratefully accepted!

The Pioneer Flight Museum is located at Old Kingsbury Aerodrome in Kingsbury, Texas. Tours are available 9:00 am-12:00 pm, and 1:00 pm-4:00 pm most weekdays and weekends. Sometimes we're tied up elsewhere, so always best to call or email ahead to ensure somebody will be available to let you in and/or show you around. Please call (830) 639-4162 or email info@pioneerflightmuseum.org
