Spring 2022

 

 

We're always looking for volunteers to help carry our mission forward, and hope you'll consider joining us! 

 

 

The next Volunteer Work Day will be on Saturday, April 9!

 

We invite you to come check out one of our regularly scheduled Volunteer Work Days here at PFM, where the gang will be tinkering with vintage airplanes and antique vehicles that always want a little attention to ensure they continue running well. A few folks tend to building and grounds issues, such as constructing an office for the car barn. It's always a good mix of fun stuff to work on and amiable camaraderie. We usually get started in the main office with coffee and goodies at 9:00 am. Just look for where the cars are parked, and follow where you see others going in.
