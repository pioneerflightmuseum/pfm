Spring 2018

 

 

We're always looking for volunteers to help carry our mission forward, and hope you'll consider joining us! 

 

The next PFM Volunteer Work Day is Saturday, April 7, 2018

Spring Wings & Wheels Fly-In is the following Saturday, April 14!

We're in the home stretch of preparing for our spring event, which promises to bring plenty of things to stay busy with here at Old Kingsbury Aerodrome: vintage airplanes and antique vehicles that need to be spiffed up, plus the usual array of other chores we "better git after" in order to present a shipshape venue for our Spring Wings & Wheels Fly-In. As always, we invite you to come join us for fun in the sun as a PFM volunteer!
